We have reserved rooms and special rates at the [Gage Suites](https://suitesatubc.com/gage-apartments-vancouver/), which is about a 10min walk on campus to The Nest venue where the conference takes place.

This [Booking Link](https://reserve.suitesatubc.com/GROUP/availability.asp?hotelCode=UBC&sdl=Check+In&startDate=03%2F25%2F2026&edl=Check+Out&endDate=03%2F30%2F2026&adults=1&children=&rooms=1&requesttype=invBlockCode&code=G260325A) will take you to a 5 night stay, checking in on Wednesday, March 25th (day before first Pre-Conference Day) and checking out on Monday, March 30th (the day after the end of the Main Conference.

If you come back to the site from elsewhere, our booking code is `G260325A`.

Adjust your dates accordingly depending on what events you’ll be participating in. As part of your registration, you’ll be prompted to select Thursday and Friday activities.

See the Thursday and Friday schedule for more information.

_Note: for those wishing to stay earlier / longer, we have the same rates 3 days before and 3 days after._

## Travel Support

Organizers, speakers, and other community members who are having their travel supported will all be staying at this location. As part of travel support onboarding, you’ll be booked into these suites.

## Room

The rooms we have reserved are their Signature Suites:

> Premium private studio room with queen bed, fully equipped kitchen with appliances, and washroom with shower (no bathtub). Superior bathroom and kitchenette finishes. Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi and LCD TV. Sleeps up to two people. Suite has air conditioning during the summer months

There are other, larger suites with a second bed available at the same location that you can review in the booking link.
